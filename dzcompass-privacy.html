DZCompass Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Overview

DZCompass is a navigation app for skydivers that helps you find your way back to the dropzone. We take your privacy seriously.

Summary: DZCompass works entirely offline. Your location never leaves your device. We collect nothing.

Data Collection

Location Data

  • What we collect: Your device's GPS location and compass heading
  • Why: To calculate direction and distance to your selected dropzone
  • Storage: Location data is processed locally on your device only. We do NOT transmit, store, or share your location with any servers or third parties.

Dropzone Data

  • What we use: A bundled database of US dropzone locations (names, coordinates)
  • Source: Publicly available dropzone directory information from official sources including the United States Parachute Association (USPA) website
  • No personal data: This data does not include any user information

Data Sharing

We do not share any data. DZCompass:

  • Does not connect to the internet
  • Does not have user accounts
  • Does not collect analytics
  • Does not contain advertising
  • Does not transmit any data off your device

Data Storage

All data remains on your device:

  • Selected dropzone preference: Stored locally via WatchConnectivity between your iPhone and Apple Watch
  • Location data: Processed in real-time, never stored

Third-Party Services

DZCompass uses no third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

DZCompass does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no data to request, modify, or delete.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
